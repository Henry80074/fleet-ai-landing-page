The Problem

Existing fleet telematics systems are data-rich but insight-poor. They generate millions of data points but lack contextual understanding, leaving fleet managers struggling to convert raw data into meaningful action.

Current Pain Points in the Market

1. Generic Scoring Systems

"Harsh braking" is penalised equally whether it happens on a motorway at high speed or on a wet rural road with poor visibility. This one-size-fits-all approach fails to distinguish between necessary defensive driving and genuinely risky behaviour.

2. No Environmental Awareness

Critical contextual factors such as weather conditions, road surface quality, lighting levels, and traffic density are completely ignored by traditional systems. This creates a blind spot in risk assessment.

3. Ineffective Coaching

Fleet managers receive mountains of alerts and reports but struggle to convert this data into behaviour change. Drivers become frustrated with seemingly arbitrary warnings that don't reflect the reality of their driving conditions.

4. Rising Insurance Costs

Despite investing in telematics monitoring, many fleets still see insurance premiums increase year-over-year. Insurers need better predictive data to justify premium reductions.

5. Compliance Pressure

Duty of care obligations and ESG reporting requirements demand proactive safety management. Basic event logging is no longer sufficient to demonstrate due diligence.

The Core Issue:

Traditional telematics tells you what happened. DriveWise AI tells you why it happened and what to do about it.

Step 3 - Intelligent Analysis: Our AI engine evaluates the true risk of each event by considering all contextual factors. A harsh brake on a motorway in clear conditions scores differently than the same event on a wet country lane.

Step 4 - Actionable Insights: Fleet managers and drivers receive clear, contextualised feedback explaining why certain behaviours were flagged and specific recommendations for improvement.
